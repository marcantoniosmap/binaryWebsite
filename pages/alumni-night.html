What is Alumni Night?

This event is mainly intended to improve and maintain relations between current undergraduate students with the alumni, in terms of both personally and professionally. To ensure such bonds can be formed, especially in regards to professional bonds, we are implementing a new system to support our objectives this year, which is a project exhibition.
